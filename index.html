How to Play

1. Draw & Match: Click the deck to reveal a target. Find it in the Center or Steal from a player!

2. Rounds: The game has 3 Rounds. Each round adds more cards and difficulty.

3. Power-ups: Get a 3x Streak to unlock a Power-up:

  • 🌀 Shuffle: Mixes the center cards.
  • 👁️ Peek: Briefly reveals all center cards.
  • 🛡️ Shield: Protects you from theft for one turn.

4. Win: Total score (collected cards) accumulates across 3 rounds. Highest total score wins!
